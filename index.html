Maharashtra Lifts all Covid Curbs

Maharashtra cabinet on Thursday cleared a proposal to withdraw the disaster management and epidemic act for Covid-19 management in the state and lifted all Covid curbs. India has reported 1,225 new Covid-19 cases and 28 deaths in the last 24 hours, the Union health ministry said on Thursday. Active caseload in the country has fallen to 14,307.
